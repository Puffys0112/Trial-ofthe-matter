Situation Briefing

A critical non-conformance alarm has triggered a facility lockdown at MediSeal's plunger production plant. The batch BN-2024-3200 — rubber plungers destined for insulin vials and diabetic injection devices — is on hold pending investigation.

As the on-call Quality Inspector, you must work through every station: verify incoming materials, complete an AQL inspection, calibrate instruments, raise a CAPA, and authorise the final batch disposition — all before the external FDA auditor arrives in 25 minutes.

Quality Topics Covered

GMP AQL / ISO 2859-1 CAPA ISO 13485 Calibration Batch Records NCR Visual Inspection Defect Classification SOP Compliance ISO 9001:2015 ISO 15378:2017 Risk-Based Thinking

How to Play

🔍 Examine hotspots in each area to find clues and collect items
🎒 Select an item from your inventory, then click a hotspot to use it
🧩 Solve puzzles — every answer is grounded in real quality principles
📋 Check Field Notes — clues and facts are recorded automatically
💬 Use Group Chat — coordinate with your team in real time
🔑 Confirm codes together — key milestones require all members to enter the code
There are 5 areas and 11 puzzles to complete in 25 minutes. Areas unlock as you progress — you cannot skip ahead.

Rules

👥 Team size: 3 – 5 members. All must be connected before the game starts.
⏱️ Simultaneous start: Every player must click "I'm Ready". The timer starts for all at the same moment.
🔑 Team confirmation: At key checkpoints the code is revealed to all — every member must type it to proceed.
🚪 Area locks: Each area is locked until the previous task is completed — complete tasks in order.
❌ Wrong answers: Each incorrect answer deducts 10 points from your final score — think before you submit.
💡 Hints: Each area has one hint available. Using a hint costs −60 seconds and −50 points. Hints are subtle — they do not give you the answer.
🏁 One attempt: Each group plays once. Results are final and posted to the leaderboard.
