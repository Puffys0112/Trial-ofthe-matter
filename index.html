THE ABANDONED LABORATORY

An Online Escape Room Experience

You wake up on the floor of a cold, dimly lit laboratory. The last thing you remember is accepting a contract job for a biotech company called NEXUS-7. The door is locked. The lights flicker. Somewhere deep in the facility an alarm is counting down…

You have 20 minutes to find the exit code and escape before the automated lockdown seals the building forever.
HOW TO PLAY
• Examine objects in each room to find clues and items
• Click items in your Inventory to select them, then use hotspots
• Notes you discover are saved in the Notes panel
• Navigate between unlocked rooms using the Rooms panel
• There are 4 rooms and 5 puzzles to solve
